This game was written using: JS, HTML, CSS.
The goal of the game is to score 100 points before the other player does.

What buttons do we have: 
ROLL DICE - to spin the cube.
HOLD - to hold the current score and pass the move.
NEW GAME - starts a new game.

Rules of the game: in his turn, the player presses the button ROLL DICE, 
gaining a certain number of points, but as soon as the number 1 is on the cube, 
the total score of points gained for this turn is reset to zero.
